​​Mission Statement

Our mission is to empower musicians and electronic music enthusiasts worldwide by delivering high-quality, sustainable modular synthesizer kits and pre-constructed parts that inspire creativity, innovation, and a deeper understanding of sound design.

Mission, Core Values & Vision

​Core Values

Quality: Crafting expertly designed, ergonomic, and reliable modules and components with an emphasis on intuitive functionality, clean aesthetics, and durable materials.
Accessibility: Making modular synthesis approachable for all skill levels through user-friendly designs, detailed documentation, and easy-to-follow assembly guides.
Education: Providing comprehensive resources to teach both East and West Coast synthesis techniques, fostering knowledge from beginner to advanced levels.
Sustainability: Prioritizing environmentally friendly practices and materials in all aspects of production.
Community: Building a supportive global network of DIY enthusiasts and electronic musicians through collaboration and shared passion for sound exploration.

Vision
To become the leading provider of DIY modular synth solutions and educational tools, recognized for excellence in design, functionality, and innovation. We aim to inspire a new generation of electronic musicians while fostering sustainability, creativity, and a thriving community. Through strategic partnerships and forward-thinking product development, we strive to set the industry standard and create a lasting impact in modular synthesis.
