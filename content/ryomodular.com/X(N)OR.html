​- Directly modelled on RTL digital logic gate designs pioneered in the 1950s used in computing equipment throughout the 50s and 60s.
- Can be used as audio waveshaper/distortion.
- The XNOR/XOR/NOT module can be patched as:
*two NOT gates and one 2-input XOR gate, one NOT gate,
*one 2-input XOR gate and one 2-input XNOR/XOR gate,
*two 2-input XNOR/XOR gates, or,
*one NOT gate and one 3-input XNOR/XOR gate.
- All analogue circuitry.
