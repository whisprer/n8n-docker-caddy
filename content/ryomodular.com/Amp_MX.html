​- 4-channel amplifying high precision mixer/attenuator.
- Works as 4 channel mixer, 1 attenuator + 3 channel mixer, 2 attenuators + 2 channel mixer or 4 attenuators.
- 2.0x gain (6dB) per channel, channels can be chained for greater gain, any combination of channels in any order. (16x gain, 24dB)
- 5.0V offset per channel.
- All analogue circuitry.
- Hi-fidelity signal chain with low noise opamps.
- High impedance inputs and buffered outputs.
